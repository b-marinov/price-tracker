## Product Strategy Flags

*As the Product Strategist, I assessed the top 3 features for commercial viability:*

**Feature 1 — Price Comparison:** This is the core value proposition of the entire product — without it, nothing else matters. Bulgarian consumers are highly price-sensitive across a competitive grocery landscape where Kaufland, Lidl, Billa, and Fantastico actively undercut each other weekly. The "cheapest first" sort with a Best Price badge is a proven UX pattern (see PriceSpy, Google Shopping) that drives repeat visits and word-of-mouth. Monetization path is clear: free tier shows current prices, premium tier unlocks price alerts and shopping list optimization. **Recommendation: Build**

**Feature 2 — Price History & Trends:** This is the feature that converts a casual visitor into a loyal power user. Seeing that olive oil was cheaper three weeks ago, or that a store raises prices before a "sale," builds trust and creates genuine consumer advocacy — users will share screenshots. It differentiates the product from simply reading the weekly flyer and is the strongest candidate for a premium tier gate (e.g., history beyond 1 month is paid). **Recommendation: Build**

**Feature 3 — Store Brochure Viewer:** Real discovery value — many Bulgarian shoppers start their week browsing brochures. However, PDF parsing (Issue #8) carries meaningful technical risk: store brochures are notoriously inconsistent, and a broken viewer damages credibility more than no viewer at all. Monetization is indirect. Defer until Price Comparison and History are solid. **Recommendation: Defer**

**Strategic Watch:** The biggest commercial risk is data reliability — if scraped prices are stale or mismatched, users will distrust the product after one bad grocery trip. Bulgarian word-of-mouth (positive and negative) travels fast.

**Opportunities:** A shopping list "split-shop optimizer" (route users to the cheapest store combination for their full basket) would be a strong premium upsell and has no direct local competitor currently. Flag as `PO-suggestion` for Boris's consideration.
